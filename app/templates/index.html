About the Application

The AI Productivity Coach is designed to help you enhance your productivity by providing insights into how you spend your time. It allows you to track tasks, categorize them, and visualize your productivity through intuitive charts.

Features

  • Task Management: Easily add, edit, and organize your tasks.
  • Time Tracking: Log start and end times for each task to understand how you allocate your time.
  • Productivity Insights: Receive detailed statistics on your productivity levels.
  • Visual Analytics: Generate charts to visualize your time spent across different categories.

Benefits

By using the AI Productivity Coach, you can:

  • Identify areas for improvement in your time management.
  • Make data-driven decisions to enhance your work habits.
  • Achieve a better work-life balance by allocating time effectively.

How It Works

Simply enter your tasks along with their categories and timeframes. The application will generate a detailed schedule, compute your productivity stats, and visualize the results through charts.
